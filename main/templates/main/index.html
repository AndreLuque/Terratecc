Ethereum/Polygon/BSC NFT Collection

We can take care of the NFT Smartv Contract (Whitelist, Shareholder Dividends, Burn mechanism, Reveal/Unreveal, Access Control, etc.), NFT Minting Websites - Front-end (Bootstrap, CSS, HTML, JS, jQuery) & Back-end (Web3js, Connection to Metamask, Minting from Website), Image & Metadata Generation Algorithm for NFT Artwork, and Upload Assets to IPFS

Ethereum/Polygon/BSC Token (ERC20/BEP20)

Give you the possibility of creating your very own Token, it can be to represent ownership of a physical asset, to provide access to a certain product or service, or even just to transfer value in a P2P Network. We have experience implementing full functionality from Basic Tokenomics to Tax Protocols, Burns, Proxy Contracts, Whitelists, Airdrops, etc.

Solana NFT Collection & Minting Website

Create a complete NFT Collection on Solana including the NFT Smart Contract with Candy Machine, the full Minting Website, Front-end & Back-end (Connection to Wallet and Minting from site).

Other Smart Contracts (NFT Staking, Governance, Lottery, Escrow, etc.)

Any process, software or interaction that you want to be constructed by a Smart Contract, we can construct it.


Smart Contract Audits

UX/UI Design
